# Ascension Levels Detail (English)

Detailed gameplay effects for Slay the Spire Ascension levels 21-100.

## Levels 1-20 (Base Game)
Base game ascension effects apply unchanged.

---

## Level 21: Elite HP Increase
**Effect**: Elite enemies have 4% more HP.

---

## Level 22: Normal Enemy HP Increase
**Effect**: Normal enemies have 4% more HP.

---

## Level 23: Elite Attack Enhancement
**Effect**: Elite enemies' attacks are enhanced.

**Details**:
- Gremlin Nob: Damage +1
- Lagavulin: Damage +2
- Sentry: Damage +1
- Book of Stabbing: Damage +1
- Gremlin Leader: Encourage Strength +1
- Taskmaster: Damage +3
- Giant Head: "It Is Time" damage +10
- Nemesis: Damage +5
- Reptomancer: Damage +2

---

## Level 24: Normal Enemy Attack Enhancement
**Effect**: Normal enemies' damage increased by 3%.

---

## Level 25: Potion System Changes
**Effect**: Potion effects are reduced.

**Details**:
- Blood Potion: Healing reduced by 5% of max HP
- Poison Potion: Poison -1
- Fire Potion: Damage -5
- Explosive Potion: Damage -2
- Weak Potion: Weak -1
- Fear Potion: Vulnerable -1
- Block Potion: Block -3
- Steroid Potion: Strength gain/loss ±1
- Speed Potion: Dexterity gain/loss ±1
- Swift Potion: Cards drawn -1
- Attack Potion: Card choices -1
- Skill Potion: Card choices -1
- Power Potion: Card choices -1
- Colorless Potion: Card choices -1
- Cunning Potion: Shivs -1
- Potion of Capacity: Orb slots -1
- Regen Potion: Regeneration -1
- Liquid Bronze: Thorns -1
- Essence of Steel: Plated Armor -1
- Distilled Chaos: Cards played -1
- Heart of Iron: Metallicize -2
- Fruit Juice: Max HP increase -2
- Snecko Oil: Cards drawn -1
- Fairy in a Bottle: Revival healing reduced by 10% of max HP

---

## Level 26: Enemy Pattern Enhancement
**Effect**: Enemy behavior patterns are enhanced.

**Details**:
- Louse (LouseNormal/LouseDefensive): Curl Up +3
- Slimes (AcidSlime/SpikeSlime): Attack patterns add +1 Slimed
- Looter: Thievery +5
- Jaw Worm (JawWorm): Gain Block +3 when gaining Strength
- Slavers (SlaverBlue/SlaverRed): Blue damage +1, Red unlimited Entangle
- Cultist: Base attack -4, Incantation Ritual +2 (or +1 if floor ≤3)
- Fungi Beast: Spore Cloud +1
- Gremlins: Various enhancements per type
- Shelled Parasite: Plated Armor +2
- Mugger: Thievery +10
- Byrd: Caw Strength +1
- Chosen: Damage +2
- Centurion: Defend Block +5
- Healer: When alone, gain Strength +2 and heal 10% HP per turn
- Snecko: Damage +2
- Snake Plant: Malleable +1
- Spheric Guardian: Base Block +15, Max HP -5
- Bandit Leader: Pointy damage +1, Bear HP +6
- Spiker: HP +5
- Exploder: HP +5
- Orb Walker: HP +6
- Darkling: HP +10
- The Maw: HP +50
- Transient: Fading +1 (fade delay +1 turn)
- Spire Growth: Damage +5
- Writhing Mass: Parasite pattern probability increased (10% → 25%)
- Giant Head: HP +100%, Slow effect changed (25% damage increase per card played)

## Level 38: Boss Heal Reduction
**Effect**: Post-boss heal reduced by 10%.

**Details**:
- Vanilla A5+: Heal 75% of missing HP
- Level 38+: Heal 65% of missing HP (additional -10%)
- Example: Max HP 80, Current HP 5 (75 missing)
  - A5-37: Heal 56 → Final 61 HP
  - A38+: Heal 48 → Final 53 HP
